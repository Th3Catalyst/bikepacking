Route Overview

"The High Cascades Overnighter is pure singletrack bliss covering everything from the high desert to mountain lakes. This route is a great tour of some of the mountain biking options around Bend, Oregon." -Rob Knoth


This route is a much more intermediate route, with a lot of climbing, and more distance. Many areas on this route can be loose and rocky, so tires wider than 3" are recommended. This route has many beautiful views, but with the altitude, you may encounter snow even up until June or July.
