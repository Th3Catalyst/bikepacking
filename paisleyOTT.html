Route Overview

"A mix of lonely paved roads, remote gravel, and high alpine singletrack. Suitable for a family bikepacking overnight, with good singletrack skills." -Dawn Rae Knoth


This is an easy to intermediate overnighter route, even if it has a short length, there are a few steep and rocky sections with a solid amount of singletrack. It is an incredibly beautiful route with many beautiful lakes, great camping options, and amazing forests. More details on how to get to the start and end of the route are linked below.
